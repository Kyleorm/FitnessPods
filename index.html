FAQ

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know before your first session.

After booking you'll receive a unique access code via SMS and email. Enter it on the keypad at your pod's entrance. No keys, no fobs, no waiting — just show up and train.

Each pod holds up to 3 people. You pay for the pod — not per person. Split a session with friends and the per-person cost becomes even better value.

Daytime sessions (open until 4:30pm) are £6.50/hr per pod. Evening sessions (4:30pm onwards) are £10/hr per pod. All prices are per pod — not per person — so you can bring up to 3 people for the same price.

No — you can book and pay per session with no commitment. Memberships save money if you train regularly. App users get their very first session completely free, no card required.

Both let you book pods. The app offers more: a free first session, push notifications for deals, saved payment details for one-tap checkout, and your full booking history in one place. The app is the better experience.

Yes. FitnessPod is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Book whenever you like and show up whenever works for you — 6am, 11pm, Christmas Day. No closing times, no restricted hours.

Yes — changes and cancellations can be made through the app or website up to 1 hour before your booking starts. Full details are available in our terms of use.

Yes — personal trainers are welcome to use the pods with their clients. Just book as normal and bring your PT along. The only rule is the standard one: no more than 3 people in a pod at any time.
