Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

FitnessPod Isle of Man ("FitnessPod", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under Isle of Man data protection law (which mirrors the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

1. Who We Are

FitnessPod IOM is the data controller for personal information collected through this website and our mobile app. You can contact us at hello@fitnesspod.im.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account data: name, email address, phone number
  • Booking data: pod selected, date, time, number of people, payment amount
  • Payment data: we do not store card details — payments are processed by PayPal or SumUp directly
  • Communications: messages you send via the contact form
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via anonymised analytics only)

3. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis)

  • Contract performance: to process your bookings and deliver your door access code
  • Legal obligation: to maintain accurate financial records
  • Legitimate interest: to improve our service and prevent fraud
  • Consent: to send you marketing communications (you can withdraw at any time)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and confirming your bookings
  • Sending your door access code via email and SMS
  • Responding to enquiries
  • Sending service updates or offers (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal requirements

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • PayPal / SumUp: to process payments (governed by their own privacy policies)
  • SMS provider: to deliver your door access code
  • Hosting providers: Vercel (website) and Supabase (database) — both process data under appropriate data processing agreements
  • Legal authorities: where required by law

6. Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active, plus 12 months after your last booking. Booking records are retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping obligations. You can request deletion at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

7. Your Rights

Under Isle of Man and UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification if your data is inaccurate
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") subject to legal retention obligations
  • Restriction of processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@fitnesspod.im. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and row-level security on our database. We never store payment card details — all card processing is handled by PayPal or SumUp.

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function (session management, security). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: anonymised usage data to improve the site. You can opt out at any time.

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy queries: hello@fitnesspod.im

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Isle of Man Information Commissioner's Office.
