Maybe you've been put off by busy gyms before — the queues for equipment, the feeling of being watched, the noise. FitnessPod is completely different. Book a pod, lock the door, and it's just you. No audience. No judgment. Just the session you actually came for.

Bring a friend.

Up to 3 people can share a pod for one flat price. Train with your partner, best mate, or sibling — split the hourly rate between you and it often works out cheaper than a standard gym membership each. Private space, your music, your pace. No strangers involved.

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before your first session.

After booking you'll receive a unique access code via SMS and email. Enter it on the keypad at your pod's entrance. No keys, no fobs, no waiting — just show up and train.

Each pod holds up to 3 people. You pay for the pod — not per person. Split a session with friends and the per-person cost becomes even better value.

Daytime sessions (open until 4:30pm) are £7/hr per pod. Evening sessions (4:30pm onwards) are £10.50/hr per pod. All prices are per pod — not per person — so you can bring up to 3 people for the same price.

No — you can book and pay per session with no commitment. Memberships save money if you train regularly.
